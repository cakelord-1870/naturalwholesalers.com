Dry Skin Pets' Shampoo (Oatmeal & Vanilla) | Natura Pet

by Natura Pets

Share this:

Share on Facebook Tweet on Twitter Share on Google+ Pin on Pinterest

Natura Pets' Oatmeal & Vanilla Dry Skin Care Pet Shampoo (495 ml) is a 100% natural shampoo for dogs, cats and horses for cracked skin, dry skin, sensitive skin.

  • Nourishes sensitive, dry skin and helps to restore natural moisture
  • Cleans safely and thoroughly with eco-friendly and cruelty-free ingredients
  • Soothes dry, itchy skin fast
  • Infuses hair with moisture and leaves the coat smooth
  • 100 % natural ingredients
  • pH value is formulated specifically for pets (pH7)
  • Free of parabens, sulphate, colorants and soap
  • Will not wash off topical flea applications

Ingredients

Aqua, Renewable coco nut-based cleanser, Renewable coco nut-based conditioner, Vegetable Glycerin, Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis), Coconut Oil (Capric Triglyceride), Shea Butter (Butyrospermum parkii), Castor Oil (Ricinus communis), Colloidal Oatmeal (Avena sativa), Mango Oil (Magnifera Indica), Vanilla Fragrance & Cinnamon Fragrance, Xantham Gum, Citric acid, Natural Preservatives (Ecocert Approved)

How to use

Wet fur with warm water. Apply shampoo to the wet fur. Rub in briskly and praise vigorously! Leave the lather to stand for some minutes to allow the natural ingredients to take effect. Rinse well and repeat if necessary. Use one of Natura Pets' conditioners for an extra healthy and glossy coat! Don’t forget to reward with one of our organic treats!

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes and ears!! Rinse thoroughly if exposed to eyes. Store in a dark, cool place.
